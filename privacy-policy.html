Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

Blue Jay ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

Blue Jay stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Food recall entries you type in
  • Your selected focus food and swap choices
  • Check-in data (swap usage, craving levels, streaks)
  • Subscription status

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. Your food and health data never leaves your device.

Subscriptions

Blue Jay offers optional premium subscriptions (monthly and yearly) managed through Apple's App Store. Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We use RevenueCat to manage entitlement status. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user ID to verify your subscription — no personal information is shared.

For details on RevenueCat's privacy practices, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Analytics and Tracking

Blue Jay does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We do not collect usage data, device identifiers, or location information.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service integrated in Blue Jay is:

  • RevenueCat — for subscription management. RevenueCat processes anonymous transaction data provided by Apple. No personal data is shared by us.

Data Storage and Security

All app data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults). If you delete the app, all local data is permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

Blue Jay is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. You can delete all app data at any time by deleting the app. To manage or cancel your subscription, go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
