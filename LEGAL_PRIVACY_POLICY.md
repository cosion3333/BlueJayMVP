# BlueJay Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-09

BlueJay ("we", "our", "us") provides a food swap coaching app for iOS.

## Data We Process

- **Food recall entries** you type in the app.
- **Weekly focus and swap usage** data used for progress tracking.
- **Subscription and purchase status** from Apple and RevenueCat.

## Where Data Is Stored

- App usage data (recall entries, focus, and swap logs) is stored locally on your device via iOS storage APIs.
- Subscription state is processed through Apple In-App Purchases and RevenueCat.

## Purchases and Billing

- Billing is handled by Apple through your Apple ID account.
- We do not directly collect or store your full payment card details.

## Analytics and Diagnostics

- BlueJay records lightweight in-app event and diagnostic logs (for example, recall completed, focus set, paywall shown, and purchase state changes) to improve product reliability and support.
- These diagnostics are limited and intended for product health monitoring.

## Data Sharing

- We do not sell personal data.
- We share data only with service providers required to run subscriptions and app functionality (for example, Apple and RevenueCat).

## Data Retention and Deletion

- Local app data stays on your device unless you delete it or uninstall the app.
- You can clear app data from within app controls when available.

## Children's Privacy

BlueJay is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Your Choices

- You can cancel subscriptions in Apple Account Settings.
- You can restore purchases in-app.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact: support@bluejayapp.com
